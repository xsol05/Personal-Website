Comp Girls Leader

Nov 2019 - Oct 2021

Comp Girls Otago is the University of Otago’s Women in STEM club. Our mission is to inspire and support women in STEM. We are a safe community and welcome all who embrace our mission, regardless of gender and background.

The events we held included:
• Career session in collaboration with the Careers Department
• Visit to local ed-tech company Education Perfect
• Speaker session with Cheryl Adams, CEO of Animation Research Ltd
• End-of-semester study groups for COSC and INFO papers

My responsibilities:
• Managed the operations and adminstration of the club to ensure smooth running
• Established the club’s social media presence which led to a 50% increase in event turnouts and member engagement
• Designed promotional materials such as posters, social media posts, and videos which generated 1,300+ impressions
• Collaborated with industry leaders to increase club awareness among companies and gained our first sponsorship

Promotional Director

Sep 2018 - Aug 2019

Malam Malaysia (Malaysian Night) is an annual cultural production organised by the Otago Malaysian Students’ Association (OMSA). Established in 1967, OMSA is one of the most active cultural associations on campus.

My responsibilities:
• Directed and supported the Design and Camera Crews to produce the videos, posters, tickets, and T-shirt
• Planned and executed the marketing campaign which led to a turn out of over 400 attendees on the night
• Collaborated with the Scriptwriters and Directors of other departments to ensure that my department created content aligned with their vision
• Worked with the 100+ cast members during the filming and shooting days to produce the content
